OUR STORY

A Nobleman to an Emperor

The founder Mr. Hari (a) Aravindan's love for cooking, cultivated at his father's hotel in Cudaloore while playing around the hotel's kitchen.

The Hotel was very successful in Cudaloore in the streets of Mela Masi Veedi. However, the ultimately death of his father brought the hotel's chapter to a close, plunging the family into financial hardship.

At the age of 14, Mr. Hari decide to earn for his family and journeyed to Erode to seek employment. With his love for food and cooking in his blood, he joined Mr. Gopal Iyer and learned proper culinary art, where his desire for cooking truly bloomed day by day.

His passion for excellent cuisine drove every step of his career, so he knew that in order to prepare the food he wanted and work for a business he was proud of, he would need to create his own catering company.

In the Year 1979, with all the blessings of Mr. Gopal Iyer and with the help of friends and well-wishers Hari Catering Service was born.

With just a bite, people felt the taste of how much we care. Soon word quickly spread and Hari Catering Service had become the most trusted provider of food for the top venues, events, and business in the city, making a nobleman, Mr. Hari an Emperor of the culinary empire.
