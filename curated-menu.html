DRIVE TO PROGRESS

Our Facilities

At Hari Catering Service, we strive for continuous progress by actively exploring and cultivating fresh ideas for our business daily, eased by our extensive facilities.

OFFICE

Where New Creations Come to Life

Our Head Office, nestled in Karungal Palayam, Erode, is the hub for our day-to-day planning and where new creations come to life.

This facility is outfitted with an In-house Kitchen capable of preparing food for up to 1,000 members at a time, as well as storage rooms to store a variety of essential and disposable items.

PRODUCTION UNIT

Stimulating & Thriving Work Space

In 2009, A Production facility was established at Thottipalayam, taking up an area of 1 Acre amidst lush surroundings.

This stimulating and thriving workspace is equipped with an in-house kitchen and storerooms for vessels, utensils, cooktops, and buffet stalls, granting us the capacity to prepare food for a maximum of 10,000 people.
